MarkDown语法

###ps: 一定要注意有空格，不然会报错

tags: 博客这里需要加空格

“＃” 一级标题

“＃＃” 二级标题

“＃＃＃” 三级标题

“>” 引用

“！［］（）“ 图片

“［］（）“ 链接

“＊＊” 粗体和 “＊”斜体

表格
“
| Tables | Are | Cool |
| ————- |:————-:| —–:|
| col 3 is | right-aligned | $1600 |
| col 2 is | centered | $12 |
| zebra stripes | are neat | $1 |”

Tables Are Cool
col 3 is right-aligned $1600
col 2 is centered $12
zebra stripes are neat $1
